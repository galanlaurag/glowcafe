About us

Glow opened in March 2017. We are a small cafe with a huge heart for both humans and animals. We’re here to feed and nourish people with the best of a plant-based menu – featuring the highest quality ingredients available, creatively crafted and made with love.

Our staff prepare and serve food as if they were serving their own families – with the highest attention to quality, detail and care. There is something for everyone! That was the vision from the beginning - regardless of your dietary requirements, there is something delicious here for you.

Our philosophy

We thrive to deliver fresh, healthy, vegan, 100% natural products, following a minimalistic approach. All dishes at our cafe are plant-based, gluten-free, and sugar-free. The majority of products that we use are locally-delivered, bio, organic, and low-processed. We want every person coming through our doors to leave inspired by plants, completely satisfied, and, most importantly, truly nourished.

We are on a mission to try and reduce the pollution of our planet. All our takeaway boxes, bags, cups, cutlery, and napkins are 100% biodegradable. We compost and recycle all the waste that we produce when working.

Our story

Glow started as a juice bar in March 2017. In June 2018 we started including more food options to our menu - smoothies, smoothie bowls, salads - all vegan and fresh. Since then we expanded our menu to some more complex plant-based dishes - keeping our initial fresh, low-processed foundation. Our philosophy remained the same throughout all of these years.

Glow is a small, local place. The restaurant has been at the same location since it opened and has been one of the rare constants in a rapidly evolving neighbourhood.
